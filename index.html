Unimontes Information

Foundation

FUNM, currently UNIMONTES, was created by State Law N°. 2,615, of May 24, 1962, authored by former deputy Cícero Dumont. In 1963, the first higher education unit in the North of Minas was created, the then Faculty of Philosophy, Sciences and Letters - FAFIL, supported by the Luiz de Paula Educational Foundation - FELP. On 13/04/63, activities of the courses of Geography, History, Letters and Pedagogy began at the premises of Colégio Imaculada Conceição.

Unimontes

Through the 1989 State Constitution, FUNM was transformed into the State University of Montes Claros, established through State Decree N°. 30.971, of 03/09/1990. The effective integration of UNIMONTES as a public entity (autarchy) occurred, however, from 01/08/1990, when the employees of the extinct FUNM began to be incorporated into the staff of the State. The university's first statute was approved through State Decree N°. 31,840, of 09/24/1990.

The State University of Montes Claros, is located Campus Universitário Prof. Darcy Ribeiro, Av. Rui Braga, s/n - Vila Mauricéia, Montes Claros - MG, 39401-089.

Funorte

Foundation

Founded by Ruy Muniz and Dean Raquel Muniz, Funorte College, with 31 years of foundation, formed in 1990. It stands out as the largest private college in the North of Minas Gerais, comprising 5 courses.

A total of 27 on-site undergraduate courses, in distance education, 44 undergraduate courses and 3 technologists, in on-site Postgraduate courses, it has 27 different specifications, therefore it is also one of the main contributors to the formation of the educational center in the city of Montes Claros -Minas Gerais.

Military Police Battalion

Information

The 10° Battalion carries out preventive and repressive policing in an area of 16,500 km², serving just over half a million people. This amount refers to half of the city of Montes Claros, 14 neighboring cities and three districts. Until the year 2010, the 10th BPM was solely responsible for policing the entire city of Montes Claros and some nearby cities. With the growth of Montes Claros and the North Region of Minas, the 50° BPM was created on 03/05/2010, which shared with the 10° BPM the responsibility in that region.

The 10° Military Police Battalion is located at: Av. Maj. Alexandre Rodrigues, 243 - Ibituruna, Montes Claros - MG, 39401-301, and is open from 8:00 am to 6:00 pm from Monday to Friday.
